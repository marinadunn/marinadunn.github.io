I am a recent graduate of the M.S. Engineering: Data Science program at University of California, Riverside, and am currently a CRESST II Research Assistant in the Planetary Systems Laboratory at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center. My interdisciplinary graduate research is collaborative with Deep Skies Lab, and focuses on Deep Learning image classification methods for upcoming large-scale astrophysics surveys like the Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST).

Research Interests: Data Science, Machine Learning, Astrostatistics, Cosmology, Galaxy Formation & Evolution
