Marina Dunn

she/her/hers

Hello! I am graduate student at the University of California, Riverside where I currently study data science with a research emphasis in astrostatistics/astroinformatics. My passions include space exploration missions, STEM inclusion, diversity, accessibility, and outreach (including advocating for women and marginalized groups in STEM), telescope operations, and data science.

I am also a Graduate Intern at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, where I currently work on migrating NASA observations, data processing workflows, and computer models to the commercial cloud.

I am also an artist in my free time, including a space artist! Feel free to check out my page and contact me for inquiries!
